FAQ

Does NothingButTyler+ have pricing, or is it free?

No, NothingButTyler+ does not have pricing, and it is free, but some restrictions will be disabled for a premium version of NothingButTyler+, which is coming soon.

Why do I have to submit a form when signing up?

We let you submit a form when signing up NothingButTyler can include this when you log in, and then staff can contact you to let you know your dashboard is ready. If you didn't submit the form, you wouldn't get access to the dashboard.

Why can't I choose a password instead of a PIN?

We make you choose a PIN instead of a password because most visitors for NothingButTyler+ don't like using passwords, so instead, we use PIN's.
